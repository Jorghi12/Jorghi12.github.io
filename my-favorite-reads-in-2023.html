My Favorite Books of 2023

In the course of this year, I immersed myself in a selection of intellectually enriching literature, delving into various works. Here are some highlights:

Top Picks

Competing Against Luck

Clayton Christensen

An insightful exploration of the Jobs to Be Done theory in innovation and business strategy.

Zero to One

Peter Thiel

A thought-provoking look at how to build companies that create new things.

Real Analysis

Walter Rudin

A comprehensive and rigorous treatment of real analysis, essential for advanced mathematics.

Professional CUDA C Programming

John Cheng, Max Grossman, and Ty McKercher

An in-depth guide to parallel programming using NVIDIA's CUDA architecture.

In Summary

These books have significantly expanded my perspective and thought processes. I've studied each book meticulously, reading them multiple times and taking extensive notes. My goal is to synthesize this knowledge and share insights on my soon-to-be-updated personal website. I encourage you to explore these works and share your own favorite reads.
