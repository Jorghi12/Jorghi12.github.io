Books · 2023

Favorite Books of 2023

In the course of this year, I immersed myself in a small set of intellectually dense books. Here are a few of the ones that stayed with me.

Top picks

Competing Against Luck

Clayton Christensen

An insightful exploration of the Jobs to Be Done theory in innovation and business strategy.

Zero to One

Peter Thiel

A thought-provoking look at how to build companies that create new things.

Real Analysis

Walter Rudin

A comprehensive and rigorous treatment of real analysis, essential for advanced mathematics.

Professional CUDA C Programming

John Cheng, Max Grossman, and Ty McKercher

An in-depth guide to parallel programming using NVIDIA's CUDA architecture.

In summary

These books pushed on innovation, company design, mathematics, and systems performance from different angles. They remain a useful snapshot of what I was thinking through that year.
