Startups I've Worked With

I've had the privilege of collaborating with nearly 20 early-stage startups, with a strong focus on the dynamic pre-seed and seed stages. These are the moments where innovation thrives, rapid iteration shapes direction, and the most valuable lessons are learned. My expertise lies in taking ideas from concept to reality—building entire products from zero to one—while seamlessly navigating every layer of the tech stack, including mobile, backend, frontend, cloud infrastructure, machine learning, and customer development.
